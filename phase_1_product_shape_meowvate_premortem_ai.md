# Phase 1 — Product Shape

## Project Name

**Meowvate PreMortem AI**

## Tagline

**Find the failure before it finds you.**

## Hackathon Category

| Item | Final Choice |
|---|---|
| Primary Category | Track 4 — Data & Intelligence |
| Secondary Fit | AI Agents with Google AI Studio |
| Optional Supporting Angle | Governance, auditability, risk scoring, decision logs |

---

## Product Type

**AI-powered enterprise failure simulation and risk intelligence platform.**

Meowvate PreMortem AI helps teams stress-test enterprise workflows before real-world incidents happen.

---

## Final Product Description

**Meowvate PreMortem AI helps enterprises find operational failures before they happen. Teams upload policies, SOPs, compliance reports, or workflow documents, choose a crisis scenario, and the AI simulates weak points, identifies risks, generates a prioritized fix plan, and creates an audit-ready trail for leadership.**

---

## Problem Statement

Enterprises usually discover workflow failures too late — during audits, incidents, customer escalations, vendor delays, or compliance reviews.

Existing tools store documents, but they do not actively stress-test operations or tell teams what will break first.

---

## Solution Statement

Meowvate PreMortem AI acts like an operational chaos simulator.

It reads enterprise documents, runs crisis simulations, detects hidden weak points, and turns risks into clear action plans before real damage happens.

---

## Target Users

| User | Why They Need It |
|---|---|
| Operations Managers | Find delays, broken handoffs, and ownership gaps |
| Compliance Teams | Prepare for audits before failure happens |
| Risk Teams | Identify business exposure early |
| Enterprise Leadership | Get executive-level risk summaries |
| Process Owners | Fix broken workflows before incidents occur |

---

## Core Demo Flow

1. User uploads or pastes an enterprise document.
2. User chooses a crisis scenario.
3. AI runs a pre-mortem simulation.
4. System detects operational weak points.
5. Failure Map shows risk score, blockers, and business impact.
6. AI generates a prioritized fix plan.
7. User approves, edits, rejects, or assigns actions.
8. Audit trail captures the full decision timeline.

---

## MVP Scope

Build only **3 screens** for the hackathon MVP.

| Screen | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Screen 1 — Scenario Simulator | Upload/paste document and choose crisis scenario |
| Screen 2 — Failure Map | Show risk score, weak points, blockers, and impact areas |
| Screen 3 — Fix Plan + Audit Trail | Show recommended actions, owners, evidence needed, and timeline |

---

## Crisis Scenarios

Use these 5 options in the app.

| Scenario | UI Label |
|---|---|
| Regulatory audit | Audit Tomorrow |
| Vendor delay | Critical Vendor Fails |
| Security incident | Sensitive Data Exposure |
| Customer escalation | Enterprise Client Escalates |
| Internal approval delay | Approval Workflow Breaks |

---

## Wow Moment

After the AI analysis, show a strong executive-style result.

Example:

> **PreMortem complete: 7 failure points detected, 3 critical blockers found, estimated operational delay risk: 14 days.**

This should appear prominently on the Failure Map screen.

---

## Core AI Output Fields

The AI should return structured data similar to this:

| Field | Example |
|---|---|
| Risk Score | 82/100 |
| Failure Points | Missing fallback approver, no escalation SLA |
| Critical Blockers | 3 |
| Estimated Delay Risk | 14 days |
| Business Impact | High compliance exposure |
| Fix Plan | Add backup approver, define escalation SLA |
| Evidence Needed | Approval matrix, audit log, escalation policy |
| Audit Events | Uploaded → analyzed → risks detected → actions generated |
